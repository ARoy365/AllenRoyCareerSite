About Me

I am Allen Roy, a Computer Science and Engineering undergraduate at VIT Chennai with a strong foundation in software development, cloud technologies, and applied machine learning. Over the years, I have worked on impactful projects ranging from AI-powered safety monitoring systems to serverless applications on AWS, and even ventured into LLM-based solutions for math comprehension. These experiences reflect my interest in combining innovation with real-world problem solving.

Alongside my technical skills in Python, Java, C/C++, SQL, and AWS Cloud services, I value creativity, teamwork, and effective communication as much as coding and algorithms. My internship experience at Vonnue Innovations sharpened my ability to design responsive, user-friendly applications under tight deadlines, while my AWS certifications reinforce my expertise in cloud computing and scalable solutions.

Outside academics and work, I enjoy exploring new technologies, experimenting with ideas that push boundaries, and continuously learning to stay ahead in this fast-evolving field. I believe in balancing professionalism with curiosity, and I aspire to use my skills to create solutions that are both technically sound and socially meaningful.
